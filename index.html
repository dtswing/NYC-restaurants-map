Visualizing NYC Restaurants

Group 12: Jeeyon Park, Siying Chen, Yu-hua Cheng, Oleksii Khomov

This project was a meterialization of our personal curiostiy about the available food options in the New York City and what we can learn about an area by looking at the local food establishment. We wanted to create an interactive data product that would give us a quick overview about the cleanliness, customer rating and cuisine types of the restaurants in NYC.
